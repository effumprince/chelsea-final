Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options and costs?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) for $5.99 and express shipping (1-2 business days) for $12.99. Free standard shipping is available for orders over $50.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unopened and unused products. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes! All Beauty Glam products are certified cruelty-free and never tested on animals.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within the United States and Canada. We're working to expand our shipping options in the future.
