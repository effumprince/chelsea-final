Product Description

Our Hydrating Facial Moisturizer is a luxurious daily moisturizer that provides intense hydration while improving skin elasticity and texture. Formulated with hyaluronic acid, aloe vera, and vitamin E, this moisturizer helps to lock in moisture and protect your skin from environmental stressors.

Perfect for morning and night use, this lightweight formula absorbs quickly without leaving a greasy residue. Suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Key Ingredients

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Attracts and retains moisture in the skin
  • Aloe Vera: Soothes and calms the skin
  • Vitamin E: Provides antioxidant protection
  • Jojoba Oil: Mimics the skin's natural oils for balanced hydration
  • Green Tea Extract: Provides antioxidant and anti-inflammatory benefits
